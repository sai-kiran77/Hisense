Pushing the boundaries of innovation since 1969

Hisense is selling products in 160+ countries. All of which have the sole aim of delivering high quality, cutting-edge and affordable products that improve the life of our consumers.

Sports Events Sponsorships

Starting from the naming rights of Hisense Arena in the Australian Open in 2008, Hisense has started a long-term sports association in line with its global brand image.

In soccer, we were the Official Sponsor of the 2018 FIFA World Cup and the Official Partner of the UEFA EURO 2016.

Hisense was also the official partner of UEFA EURO 2020. In the 60th year of the tournament, this is the second time Hisense joined in to be a global partner among other UEFA sponsors following a successful UEFA EURO 2016 campaign.

Besides this, Hisense proudly announces the partnership with Paris Saint-Germain that was launched in August 2020. This sees the Hisense product range showcased to audiences in the Parc des Princes stadium. Through this partnership, Hisense will also provide a wide range of innovative products to the club to support its needs across its various facilities.

Hisense has also been the official Premium Partner of FC Schalke 04. In F1 & car racing, Hisense has been the Team Supplier to the Red Bull Racing FI Team and has also been the Title Sponsor of the Hisense 300 NASCAR Xfinity Series & Team Sponsor of Joe Gibbs Racing.

The soccer association continues with Hisense being the sponsor of the FIFA World Cup, Qatar 2022. At Hisense, we’re glad to be on board with such a fabulous tournament and to be able to bring fans and Hisense product owners closer to the action. The 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar is set to run from November 21 to December 18, 2022.
